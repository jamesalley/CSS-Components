CSS Components

These are 'built graphics' made entirely from CSS, no imgages. They are therefore resolution independent, scalable, lightweight, and Retina-display ready. Also, they are more malleable than an image. You can easily tweak the colors, animation timing, etc. to suit your purpose.

This collection continues to grow over time so watch this project on Github.
